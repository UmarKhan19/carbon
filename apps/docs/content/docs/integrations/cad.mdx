## Onshape

Connect to **Onshape** over **OAuth** to bring CAD data into Carbon. Authorizing opens an Onshape popup;
once you approve read access, Carbon stores the connection and can pull from your Onshape documents.

| Setting | What it controls |
| --- | --- |
| Connection | Established by OAuth — no keys to paste. Carbon stores the access and refresh tokens for you. |

<Callout type="warn">
Onshape only appears when its OAuth client is configured server-side (`ONSHAPE_CLIENT_ID`) — see
[Environment variables](/docs/platform/environment-variables).
</Callout>
